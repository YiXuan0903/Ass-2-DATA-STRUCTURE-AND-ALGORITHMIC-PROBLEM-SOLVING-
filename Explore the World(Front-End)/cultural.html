Diwali - Festival of Lights (India)

Diwali is celebrated across India with fireworks, sweets, prayers, and lighting oil lamps. It signifies the victory of light over darkness.

When: October-November (date varies each year)

Where: Throughout India, especially in cities like Delhi, Mumbai, and Varanasi

Highlights: Lighting lamps, bursting fireworks, and family gatherings.

Chinese New Year (China)

Chinese New Year is celebrated with dragon dances, lanterns, fireworks, and feasts. It marks the beginning of the lunar new year.

When: January-February (date varies each year)

Where: China, Hong Kong, Singapore, Malaysia

Highlights: Lantern festivals, family reunions, and red envelopes.

Video
Rio Carnival (Brazil)

Rio Carnival is the biggest carnival in the world, featuring samba parades, street parties, and vibrant costumes.

When: February-March (dates vary)

Where: Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

Highlights: Samba parades, street parties, and extravagant costumes.
