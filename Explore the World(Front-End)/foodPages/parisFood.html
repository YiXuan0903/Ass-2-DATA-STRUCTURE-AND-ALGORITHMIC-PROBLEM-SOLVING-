Popular Dishes in Paris

Crêpes and Galettes

Sometimes, less is more, especially when it comes to the iconic French crêpes and galettes, made from a simple batter of flour, water, and salt. French onion soup

This hearty dish features caramelized onions simmered in a rich beef broth, creating a deep, flavorful base. It is then topped with a thick layer of melted cheese and a slice of crispy bread, making it the ultimate comfort food that you simply must try.

The classic French baguette

The trip to Paris would be incomplete without tasting the classic French baguette. This crispy, golden bread is a symbol of French baking, and Parisian bakeries compete to make the best baguette.
