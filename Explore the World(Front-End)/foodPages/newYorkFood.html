Popular Dishes in New York

Bagel with Cream Cheese and Lox

This is a combination of cream cheese, smoked salmon, capers, tomatoes, and red onions, all piled onto a bagel. Today, bagels have become an essential part of New York City's culinary scene.

Pastrami on Rye

Pastrami on rye is deeply rooted in Jewish deli traditions, embodying New York’s vibrant culinary scene. The combination of tender, smoky pastrami with bold mustard on hearty rye bread is a timeless classic, originating from iconic delis.

Cronut

In the spring of 2013, French pastry genius Dominique Ansel revolutionized the pastry world at his SoHo bakery. It was then that he came up with the idea of combining a croissant and a donut. Thus, the Cronut dubbed the "mad scientist" of pastries was born.
