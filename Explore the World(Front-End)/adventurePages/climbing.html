Rock Climbing

El Capitan, USA

El Capitan is one of the most iconic rock climbing spots in the world, located in Yosemite National Park. Its sheer granite faces are a challenge for climbers.

The Dolomites, Italy

The Dolomites in northern Italy are a stunning rock climbing destination, known for their towering limestone peaks and dramatic landscapes. Offering routes for all levels, climbers can explore famous spots like the Tre Cime di Lavaredo. With breathtaking views and challenging climbs, it’s a top spot for outdoor enthusiasts.

Santorini, Greece

Santorini is famous for its beautiful white-washed architecture, but it's also home to stunning cliffs perfect for rock climbing enthusiasts.
