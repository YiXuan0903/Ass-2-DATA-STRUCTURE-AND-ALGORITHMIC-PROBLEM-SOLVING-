Hiking and Trekking

Gunung Kinabalu, Malaysia

Gunung Kinabalu is the highest peak in Southeast Asia, located in Sabah, Malaysia. The trek to the summit is a challenging and rewarding experience with breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes.

Inca Trail, Peru

The Inca Trail leads to the ancient Incan city of Machu Picchu. A multi-day trek through breathtaking mountain scenery, lush jungle, and impressive archaeological sites. This trek is one of the most famous in the world.

Everest Base Camp, Nepal

The Everest Base Camp trek is an iconic adventure that takes you through the heart of the Himalayas, offering magnificent views of Mount Everest and the surrounding peaks. This trek is a true test of endurance.
