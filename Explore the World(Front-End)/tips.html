Always visit local tourism information center of the country

  • Tourism information center know about everything in the town.
  • They will point you to the free activities and their local special event.
  • They may offer some discounts on avtivities and transportation.

Carry basic first-aid kit

  • They may have some unexpected injuries during the travel and local clinics might too far or not open.
  • Bring medicine to avoid stomach pain, headache, fever or allergies.
  • Besides, get vaccinated before going another countries to avoid illness.

Try local food

  • Try to discover local food like local street food so that can experience local culture.
  • Other countries' cooking style and ingredients may not be same with own your country and this is an opportunity to try new flavors.
  • Try other countries' local food can stand a chance to learn their stories and histories.

Having a good mood

  • Having an unforgettable trip and memories so that can have a great experience on travel trip.
  • After having an amazing trip, then only hava a good mood and energy to continue the work.
  • So do not think or worry about the work, just enjoy the trip.
